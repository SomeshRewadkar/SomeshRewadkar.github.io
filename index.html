Research & Publications

"Leveraging Language Models and Technical Analysis for Real-Time Crypto Trading Signal Generation"

Presented at: IEEE International Conference Automatics and Informatics'2025 (ICAI'25).

This paper presents CryptoGPT, a novel system that integrates GPT-4, real-time crypto data from the Binance API, and over 150 technical indicators. By processing user-defined preferences like risk tolerance and strategy, CryptoGPT generates personalized trading signals, achieving a 75% success rate and outperforming traditional deep learning models.

"N-Queens using Genetic Algorithm: A Comparative Study"

Presented at: IEEE International Conference Automatics and Informatics'2025 (ICAI'25).

This study provides a comparative analysis of the traditional backtracking method against a genetic algorithm (GA) for solving the N-Queens problem. The findings demonstrate that the GA offers superior efficiency and scalability, successfully computing solutions for large board sizes (N) where the exponential time complexity of backtracking becomes computationally infeasible.
