Research Interest

Explainable AI (XAI)

I am fascinated by the challenge of making "black box" models transparent and understandable. My interest lies in developing and applying techniques like LIME and SHAP to interpret model decisions, enhance trust, and ensure fairness in critical applications such as healthcare and finance. Unpacking the 'why' behind a prediction is as crucial as the prediction itself.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

My passion for NLP stems from the desire to bridge the communication gap between humans and machines. I am particularly interested in the architecture and behavior of Large Language Models (LLMs), exploring areas like semantic understanding, text generation, and information retrieval to build more intuitive and intelligent systems.
